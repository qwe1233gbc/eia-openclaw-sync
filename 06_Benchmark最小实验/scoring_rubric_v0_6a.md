# Scoring Rubric v0.6a

## common_score_6 (A/B/C all groups)
- judgement_correct: 0-2
- evidence_use: 0-2
- actionability: 0-2

## knowledge_use_score_4 (B/C only)
- standard_basis: 0-2
- experience_basis: 0-2

## total_score_10 (B/C only)
total = common_score_6 + knowledge_use_score_4

## Comparison Rules
- B-A: compare common_score_6 + standard_basis
- C-B: compare experience_basis + review quality change
- Negative control: excluded from experience increment stats
